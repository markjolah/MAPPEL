class  mappel::PoissonNoise1DObjective
 A base class for 1D objectives with Poisson read noise. This objective function and its subclasses are for models where the only source of noise is the "shot" or "counting" or Poisson noise inherent to a discrete capture of phontons given a certain mean rate of incidence on each pixel. More...
